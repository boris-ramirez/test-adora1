Welcome

The 27th Annual Conference of the International Environment Forum (IEF) will coincide with two United Nations milestones that will shape the future of our planet this September in New York:

- the Sustainable Development Goals Summit i a global progress review of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) with new commitments to accelerated action toward target year 2030.

- and the Preparatory Ministerial Meeting for the Summit of the Future. i an agenda to reshape and strengthen global mechanisms for collective action including the United Nations itself.

To contribute to these processes the International Environment Forum, in partnership with the Adora Foundation, welcomes you to a series of panel discussions and interactive sessions to share what has been learned and contribute to action on next steps.
